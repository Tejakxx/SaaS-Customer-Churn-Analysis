# SaaS-Customer-Churn-Analysis
🔸Conducted end-to-end SaaS customer churn analysis using Python, SQL, and Power BI to analyze customer behavior, engagement, revenue (MRR/ARR), and retention patterns across subscription plans, industries, and regions.
🔸Performed statistical analysis, SQL-based business querying, and Python EDA to identify churn drivers through customer usage, satisfaction, support tickets, auto-renewal, upgrades/downgrades, and forecasting of signup & churn trends.
🔸Developed interactive Power BI dashboards with KPI tracking, churn insights, engagement analysis, and forecasting visuals to support data-driven decision-making, customer retention, and revenue optimization.
