# SaaS-Customer-Churn-Analysis
I performed an end-to-end Data Analytics project on SaaS Customer Churn Analysis to understand customer retention, engagement behavior, revenue patterns, and churn risk factors. The project focuses on transforming raw SaaS subscription and usage data into actionable business insights for improving customer retention and recurring revenue.
Key Highlights:

🔸Analyzed customer churn trends to identify retention and attrition patterns.
🔸Explored customer engagement behavior using usage count, usage duration, and platform activity.
🔸Identified high-performing industries and countries contributing to higher revenue.
🔸Studied the impact of auto-renewal, trials, upgrades, and downgrades on churn behavior.
🔸Performed revenue analysis (MRR & ARR) across plans, industries, and customer segments.
🔸Evaluated customer satisfaction, support tickets, and error counts to uncover service-related churn factors.
🔸Created forecasting models to predict future customer signups and churn trends.
🔸Designed interactive Power BI dashboards for business overview, engagement analysis, and forecasting insights.

Features Used:

🔸Data Cleaning & Preprocessing.
🔸Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
🔸Descriptive Statistical Analysis.
🔸SQL-based Business Query Analysis.
🔸Customer Behavior & Churn Analysis.
🔸Forecasting & Trend Analysis.
🔸Interactive Dashboarding & Visual Storytelling in Power BI.

Tools Used:

Python | NumPy | Pandas | Matplotlib | Seaborn | SQL | Power BI
